APEX COACHING CLASSES - FAQ

Q: What courses do you offer?
A: We offer courses for JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET, Class 11th and 12th (PCM/PCB), and Foundation courses for Class 8th, 9th, and 10th.

Q: What are the batch timings?
A: Morning batch: 6 AM - 9 AM. Evening batch: 4 PM - 7 PM. Weekend batch: 9 AM - 1 PM (Saturday and Sunday only).

Q: What is the fee structure?
A: JEE/NEET full course: Rs 45,000 per year. Class 11th/12th: Rs 30,000 per year. Foundation course: Rs 20,000 per year. Fee can be paid in 2 installments.
